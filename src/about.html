Our Story

It all started during lockdown, at home, in our kitchen. With a bit more time on our hands, we had a real go at making some pizza. The passion was there, so we kitted out our kitchen with a cracking piece of kit called the Roccbox oven. Before we knew it, we were dishing up our homemade pizzas to neighbours, family and friends. The word started to spread around town and we thought, "Why not share our pizza making escapades on social media?" So that's what we did - Instagram, Facebook, the lot. The response was pure magic. We were selling out nearly every weekend, with people driving miles to get a slice of the action.

An opportunity

That's when it hit us. This could be more than just a way to pass the time during lockdown. We could really make a go of this. So, we decided to turn our newfound passion into a proper business. As soon as lockdown lifted, we were off. We spent a brilliant summer travelling all over Ireland, serving up our pizzas at events and pubs. Wherever we went, people couldn't get enough.

Our New Home

Then, the opportunity of a lifetime came knocking - a chance to set up shop in Tullys, Carlow. We've been here ever since, doing what we love most - making top-notch pizzas and serving them to the best customers we could ask for. It's been a great journey, and we're only just getting started. We're so thankful for the support of all our amazing customers.

Fancy a taste of our homemade pizzas? Pop over to Tullys, Carlow or follow our adventures on Facebook and Instagram. See you soon!
